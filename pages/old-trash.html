The Unknown (2019)

The Unknown is an experimental horror game. You control Jacob, a young adult that has several mental health problems. Some days ago Jacob's brother died, but he doesn't feel any grief about it.

My first game, made with Unity this time, it's just a trash'y PT clone with a twist. Funningly enough i still believe 13-year-old Lucas was onto something with this and i still grab some ideas and inspos from this project to this day.
